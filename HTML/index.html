Sections

Sections are logical divisions of content within an article.

Grouping Content

Sections can group multiple pieces of related information.

They can also contain multiple article elements when needed.

<article>
  <h2>Article Title</h2>
  <section>
    <h3>Section Title</h3>
    <p>Section content</p>
  </section>
</article>

Related Content

This sidebar can hold links, notes, or supporting information.

Asides can also hold links to additional resources.

  • MDN Web Docs: <aside>
  • W3C HTML5.2: <aside>
<aside>
    <h2>Related Content</h2>
    <p>This sidebar can hold links, notes, or supporting information.</p>
    <ul>
        <li><a href="https://example.com">Related
        resource</a></li>
    </ul>
</aside>

Other Important Elements

  • <section> for related topics
  • <article> for standalone content
  • <figure> + <figcaption> for media
